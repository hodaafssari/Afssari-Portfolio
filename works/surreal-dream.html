A Surreal Dream

2023 | A-Frame

A collaborative project exploring dream spaces where opposite environments entangle mysteriously. Created in A-frame, this interactive virtual space connects different dreamlike landscapes through bridges, representing the abstract connections in our dreams. Features ambient galaxy sounds and randomly placed objects to enhance the surreal atmosphere.

When we dream, we access a space opposite of reality. Anything can happen, and nothing is impossible. Dreams connect irrelevant things in mysterious, unexplainable ways.

The project uses pastel colors, relaxing sounds, and interconnected landscapes to create a comfortable dream world that visitors can physically interact with through virtual space. Each element works together to create either a happy, comfortable atmosphere or, to others, a potential nightmare.

Purpose: To create connection and interaction with dream spaces, and to provide a relaxing environment where visitors can explore and make their own connections in ways only they can understand.
